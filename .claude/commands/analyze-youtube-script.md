### Phase 2: Deep Script Analysis

**A. Hook Analysis (First 30 seconds)**
- Identify the specific hook techniques used:
  - Pattern interrupts: Breaking expectations
  - Question hooks: Provoking curiosity
  - Story hooks: Emotional investment
  - Value hooks: Promising specific outcomes
  - Controversy hooks: Challenging common beliefs

**B. Structure Breakdown**
- **Introduction (0:00-0:30)**: Hook setup and value proposition
- **Problem Identification**: What pain points are addressed?
- **Solution Framework**: How is the main content structured?
- **Story Arcs**: Emotional journey mapping
- **Call-to-Action**: Placement and effectiveness

**C. Retention Engineering Elements**
- **Pattern Interrupts**: Moments that break viewer predictability
- **Curiosity Gaps**: Unresolved questions that keep watching
- **Emotional Pacing**: High/low energy moments and their placement
- **Visual Cues**: Script elements that suggest specific visual needs
- **Audio Design**: Moments requiring specific sound effects or music

**D. Psychological Triggers**
- **Social Proof**: Mentions of statistics, testimonials, authority
- **Scarcity**: Limited time offers, exclusive information
- **Reciprocity**: Value given before asking for engagement
- **Authority**: Credentials, expertise demonstration
- **Consistency**: Building on previously established ideas

### Phase 3: Competitive & Content Gap Analysis

**A. Similar Content Research**
- Search for: "$ARGUMENTS similar youtube videos high retention"
- Analyze top 3-5 competing videos for:
  - Hook strategies
  - Structure differences
  - Engagement patterns
  - Comment sentiment analysis

**B. Opportunity Identification**
- Content gaps in the niche
- Underserved audience segments
- Format innovations that could work
- Cross-pollination opportunities with other niches
